Alyssa Unell

I'm Alyssa, a junior at MIT studying course 6-9 (Computational Neuroscience). Currently, I am exploring the impact of noise in image classification robustness with Professor Pawan Sinha and Dr. Xavier Boix Boisch.

Previously, I created an algorithm to quantify motor control improvement using computer vision with Professor Sinha. I also contributed to an analysis of state-of-the-art facial recognition neural nets with Dr. Kyle Keane in the MIT Quest for Intelligence. Additionally, I have worked as a Machine Learning Intern for Intel serving to improve their optimization software. I have also worked as a Computational Biology Intern for the Translational Genomics Research Institute where I developed a novel system to help improve efficiency of experiments.
